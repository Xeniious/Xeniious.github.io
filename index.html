Palette

In a black and white world where color has never been known, a girl adventuring through her hometowns forest finds a magical paint gun. This gun has the power to bring colors never before seen by anyone, and with these colors comes wacky side effects! In this 2D platformer, you apply paints to the world to bring life and color to it while solving puzzles so you can move forward. Progress through to discover new paints such as the speed boosting paint, floaty paint, and much more!
